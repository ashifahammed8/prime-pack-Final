About Us

We are a young packaging company with three decades of experience. Established in the year 2012 at Prathipadu, Guntur district, Andhra Pradesh, India.

Prime pack industries were envisioned to provide complete packaging solutions for its clients. PPI specializes in the design, manufacturing, and supply of all kinds of 'Corrugated Boxes' and 'Woven Sacks'. PPI has expanded its services nationally providing its services to Textile, Agri, Tobacco, FMCG, Cement and other industries. Being a customer-centric and innovation-driven company, we have successfully grown by 20% YOY delivering value to our customers year on year.

OTHER ORIGINS

In 1975 Mr. D. Baburao had started ALLIED PACKAGINGS in collaboration with WADPACK Pvt. Ltd. It was the pioneer in introducing Heavy Duty Corrugated Box for packing tobacco. The industry had served the region for last three decades with quality and consistency. Allied Packagings had built a reputed list of clients in the tobacco industries.

In 2006 Mr. D. Baburao's nephew Mr. K.Srujan Kumar a postgraduate in Business administration and Foreign Trade had joined the company as a Business Development Executive. With a clear vision and thorough focus on research and development, he orchestrated the expansion for the business to provide services for other markets like textile, Poultry, FMCG, Parma, and others.

2012, PPI was promoted by Mr. K.Srujan Kumar as one of its kind integrated packaging solutions provider. Since its inception it has grown multifold, reaching new industries and markets. PPI today renders its services across the nation with strong support and presence in the regional market. PPI plans to expand its reach nationally in the coming years by being a listed company and create value for all its stakeholders.

Infrastructure

Corrugated Boxes

At PPI we have a high-Speed semi-automatic machine for making corrugated board and boxes of all kinds (high and low strength cartons). The Machine is equipped to produce Multiple Profiles Like:
A-Flute (3ply), B-Flute (3ply), C-Flute (3ply), A/B-Flute (5ply), A/C-Flute (5ply), B/C-Flute (5ply), and B/B-Flute (5ply).

  • High speed six color flexor printer
  • High speed Creaser slotted
  • Dye Punch Machine
  • Stitching Machines
  • Box Drying Machine
TESTING EQUIPMENTS FOR CORRUGATED BOXES:
  • GSM Tester (paper and Board)
  • Caliper Tester (paper and Board)
  • Burst Strength Tester (paper and Board)
  • Ring Crush Tester (paper)
  • Moisture Tester (paper and Board)
  • Edge Crush Tester (Board)
  • COBB Tester (paper and Board)
  • Flat crunch Tester (Board)
  • Box Compression Tester (Box)

10,000 tons of Corrugated boxes annually.

Woven Sacks

At PPI we have one of its kind infrastructure set up in the region to produce high-quality woven sacks of all types (P.P, H.D.P.E, B.O.P.P, Paper Laminated, Leno, and others). Our Key Infrastructure for woven sack production is:

  • High speed Multicolor Flexor Print Machine with Rotating Gusset (1st of its kind in Andhra Pradesh)
  • High speed Sack Cutting and Stitching Machines (Automatic)
  • Sack cutting machines (Manual)
  • Sack Stitching machines (Manual)
  • Bale Pressing Machines
TESTING EQUIPMENTS FOR WOVEN SACKS:
  • Electronic weighing scale
  • Tensile strength testing Machine
  • Denier testing machine
  • Gauge meter & GSM testing equipment
  • Viscosity Testing Machine

20 million woven sacks every year.

The facility has integrated staff residential quarters and full power backup to ensure uninterrupted production.

As a Technology Savvy and Employee Centric Company. PPI constantly have been building the internal capacity in terms of both technology and human resource to meet the growing demands of the user. Our Values

In PPI we dream of supporting Indian industries globally by becoming their preferred choice of packaging partners. As the nation grows as the new face of global manufacturing and production, we pledge to support our customers in their growth by protecting their hard work. Our progress is rooted in our value system. As we shall grow we may rewrite our rules but will strive to retain our values. Values of quality, punctuality, affordability, sustainability, adaptability, and family. These are not just words but a way of life in PPI.

Quality

Our process follows strict quality control and is assisted by an in-house quality inspection setup to ensure the best value solution for our clients both in hard and soft packaging depending on their needs. We ensure the use of best raw materials and processes. We certify our deliveries with a quality report.

Sustainability

We use environment-friendly practices to create sustainable solutions. We also ensure the cultural and financial sustainability for the people associated with us. We follow best labor practices and fair trade policies. We are a child labor free and women-friendly workspace.

Family

The most significant and important value of our organization. We believe that all our stakeholders, clients and employees form the integrated PPI family. And in each one's growth lies the key to our growth. This has been the most important yardstick of our business. We strive to work hard for the growth and expansion of our family. We wish for you to be a part of it.

Punctuality

We are committed to on-time delivery and have deployed specially designed work flow process and systems to ensure on-time delivery. We are always ready to push ourselves to help you with on-time solutions.

Affordability

In a highly competitive and technical industry. We constantly work towards improving our process and solutions to make the solution affordable to our clients. We do not cut cost to compromise on quality but ensure affordability through transparency and innovation.

Adaptability

In a constantly evolving business, we keep ourselves up to date with the latest and the best in class technology. Our staff is constantly trained and informed. With the help of our design and innovation team we constantly challenge ourselves to provide better solutions.
